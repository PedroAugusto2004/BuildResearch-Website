01 — Our story

Why we created BuildResearch

We began as a group of experienced researchers with diverse industry backgrounds — people who have taught research, led research and thought-leadership functions, and spent their careers helping organisations make sense of complex issues so they can project their analysis into the public sphere and build profile and reputation.

Over time, we realised we shared a common belief: that high-quality research and thought leadership can create many different forms of value for organisations when it is done well.

We came together to use our experience and talents to help others create research that genuinely adds value, and to help people see research not as a compliance exercise but as a strategic, value-creating activity.

That belief — and the desire to make research a practical outcome for others — is why we created BuildResearch.

02 — How we work

Our values

02

Evidence with Purpose

We believe research should make a meaningful difference — to organisations, to society, and to people's lives. We are committed to insight that is rigorous, relevant and capable of driving real change.

Clarity & Integrity

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of methodological and ethical integrity, communicating with clarity, transparency and honesty so that evidence is always trustworthy and accessible.

Capability that Endures

We strengthen the research confidence and competence of the teams we work with — leaving organisations better equipped to design, make, manage and communicate research themselves.

Partnership & Collaboration

We work alongside our clients as strategic partners. We listen deeply, challenge constructively, and co-create solutions that reflect each organisation's context, ambition and constraints.

Ambition for Better

We are driven by a commitment to continuously improve our methods, our thinking and the research culture of those we support — helping clients raise standards, sharpen strategy and elevate impact.

Insight that Moves People

We believe research should inspire action. We translate complex evidence into compelling narratives that shape decisions, strengthen reputation, and position organisations as credible, influential voices in their fields.
