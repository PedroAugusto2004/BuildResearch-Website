01

Research & Thought Leadership: Design & Delivery

We offer a full-service research and thought leadership practice that delivers rigorous qualitative and quantitative, evidence-based outcomes for organisations — from first framing to final publication and launch.

We design studies that stand up to scrutiny and tell a story that travels: research that earns attention, shapes the conversation, and positions you as a credible, influential voice in your field.

  • Research strategy & design
  • Qualitative & quantitative methods
  • Flagship reports & thought leadership
  • Surveys, interviews & analysis
  • Data storytelling & visualisation
  • Launch, dissemination & impact
02

Consulting

We use our collective research competencies and experience to offer a range of consulting engagements that support organisations wishing to re-vitalise their existing research function — or build new research capabilities from the ground up.

We work alongside your teams as strategic partners: assessing where you are, defining where you want to be, and putting the processes, standards and structures in place to get you there.

  • Research function reviews
  • Capability & maturity assessment
  • Operating models & processes
  • Quality standards & governance
  • New research function set-up
  • Strategy & roadmap
03

Coaching & Development

We provide a range of coaching options to support research development and education within organisations — a full spectrum of research coaching and development for individuals and teams.

Our goal is to help people develop, enhance and achieve higher levels of success and impact with their research activities and operations — leaving teams more confident, capable and self-sufficient.

  • 1:1 researcher coaching
  • Team capability programmes
  • Methods & skills training
  • Writing & publishing support
  • Mentoring & supervision
  • Research leadership development
